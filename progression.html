    📚 Collect Knowledge Cards

    As you play, you'll unlock fun digital safety tips and cybersecurity facts. These collectible cards make learning rewarding and memorable.

    • 💡 Never reuse the same password across platforms

      • Never reuse the same password across websites or apps. If one gets compromised, the rest could fall like dominos! → Stay unique, stay secure.

    • 🔍 Always check for HTTPS before entering credentials

      • Always make sure the site starts with https:// and has a 🔒 lock icon before entering sensitive info. → A secure connection keeps data snoops out.

    • 🎲 Use passphrases instead of single words

      • Use passphrases like “PizzaLoves#Rain23” instead of single words. They’re easier to remember — and 100x harder to guess! → Creativity is your best defense.
